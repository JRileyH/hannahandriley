Beautiful 12 mile trail on the Ashokan Reservoir, easy/flat, and great for walking or biking.

Kaaterskill Falls

Hike from North South Lake to the viewing platform for Kaaterskills falls! An easy hike, but $10 for a day pass to get into North/South Lake. While you're there you can rent kayaks or go for a swim.
