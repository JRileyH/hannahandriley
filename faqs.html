What kind of activities/events should I pack for?

For the city, if you're family, the dinner will be semi formal, but the after hang will be at a random bar so if you're not family, no need to dress up (but feel free to join us in dressing up!). Upstate, there is a pool on the grounds (might be too cold to swim though), and we will be hanging outside a lot so pack some good outdoor clothing (espcially some warm clothing because it gets cool at night)! The rail exploreres happens rain or shine so bring a rain jacket. And if you are driving upstate and have a camp chair you can bring with you, please do! There's a bonfire outside each night with some chairs and we'll have some foldable chairs, but your own camp chair might be more comfortable :)

Breakfast is provided by the hotel, lunch and dinner will be catered, and we will be providing snacks and water/seltzer/some sode, as well as beer/cider/hard seltzer in the evenings, but if there's anything else you'd like to bring for yourselves, feel free!
